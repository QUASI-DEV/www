Step 2 : Register on Bity

In Order to Invest using US-Dollar, Euro, CHF or GPB, we recommend you to coninue on the Bity-Site, where they have prepared a special workflow for the crowdfunding.

So when you go there take these steps:

  1. Go to BiTy
  2. Create new account (or use an existing)
  3. Login
  4. enter the phone number
  5. Generate or use an existing ETH-Adress
  6. Follow the Workflow
  7. Set Amount you want to invest
  8. Select bank transfer
  9. And then send the amount to the given bank account
